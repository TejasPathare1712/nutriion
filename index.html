Keto Diet
What is the Keto Diet?

The Keto Diet is a low-carbohydrate, high-fat diet that shifts the body’s metabolism into a state called ketosis, where it burns fat for fuel.

Benefits:
  • Promotes weight loss.
  • Improves mental focus and energy levels.
  • Stabilizes blood sugar levels.
Example Meal Plan:
  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with avocado.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with olive oil dressing.
  • Dinner: Salmon with asparagus sautéed in butter.
Vegan Diet
What is the Vegan Diet?

The Vegan Diet eliminates all animal products, focusing entirely on plant-based foods.

Benefits:
  • Improves heart health.
  • Reduces risk of chronic diseases.
  • Supports environmental sustainability.
Example Meal Plan:
  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with almond milk and fresh berries.
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas and tahini dressing.
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables.
High Protein Diet
What is the High Protein Diet?

This diet emphasizes protein-rich foods to aid in muscle building, repair, and metabolism support.

Benefits:
  • Supports muscle growth and recovery.
  • Promotes satiety and weight management.
  • Boosts metabolic rate.
Example Meal Plan:
  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with granola and honey.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with sweet potatoes and broccoli.
  • Dinner: Beef steak with quinoa and green beans.
